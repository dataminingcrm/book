Book Goal:
Pragmatic approach to data mining customer relationship management (CRM) data.

3 Audiences:
Financial Decision Maker (FDM): Chapters tagged with FDM provide insights to those with fiscal and fiducial responsibility; help define ROI of data mining projects.
Business Decision Maker (BDM): Chapters tagged with BDM help Analysts and business leaders understand the capabilities of DM and how to setup DM projects for success.
Technical Decision Maker (TDM): TDM chapters provide CIOs, Engineers with practical open source tools and recipes for implementing data mining and machine learning infrastructure and processes.
